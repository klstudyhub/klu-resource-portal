📖 About KL Study Hub
KL Study Hub was built around one idea — make academic resources easier to find for every student.

During exams, students struggle to find the right materials in time. This platform solves that by organizing resources in one place, making learning more efficient and less stressful.

Developed by students of KL University (Y25), we built something that benefits every student who needs quick, reliable access to study materials.
