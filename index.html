📖 About KL Study Hub
KL Study Hub was created with a simple idea — to make academic resources easier to access for every student.

During exams, students often struggle to find the right materials at the right time. This platform is built to solve that problem by organizing useful resources in one place, making learning more efficient and less stressful.

Developed by students of KL University (Y25), KL Study Hub reflects our effort to create a system that benefits not just us, but every student who needs quick and reliable access to study materials.

Our goal is to simplify learning, save time, and support students in achieving better results through shared resources.

Built by students, for students.
